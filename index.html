Ethan's First Trip to Universal Studios

Ethan had never been to Orlando before. So I convinced him to spend $5,000 on a 7-day trip. Worth it.

Visiting the Parks

Our hotel was about a 15 minute walk from the parks: Universal Studios and Islands of Adventure. We started out at Islands of Adventure, because it has out favorite rides: Harry Potter and Jurassic Park.

A Day at Disneyworld

No trip to Orlando is complete without a trip to Epcot. We attempted the "drink around the world" challenge, but unfortunately, we only made it to 8 out of the 10 countries.
